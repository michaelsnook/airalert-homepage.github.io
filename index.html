VEHICULAR EMISSION

In dense cities, vehicular emissions is one of the major causes of air pollution. The emissions of particulate matter has been linked with rising incidence of lung cancer and sustained exposure to high concentrations can lead to premature deaths.

FUELWOOD + BIOMASS BURNING

In rural India, fuelwood and biomass are used for cooking and heating purposes. This is the primary reason for for near-permanent haze and smoke observed above rural and urban India.

FUEL ADULTERATION

Many auto rickshaws and taxis use adulterated fuel to save on costs. These emit unsafe pollutants which further deteriorate the quality of air.
